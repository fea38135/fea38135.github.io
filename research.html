Research Interests

My main focus currently lies on investigating questions regarding the inverse kinematics, cuspidality, symmetries and singularities of serial robots with algebraic and geometric approaches. In particular, this includes techniques of algebraic topology and geometry, Galois and monodromy groups, homotopy continuation and Morse theory.

More broadly I am interested and sometimes think about topics related to persistent homology, computational topology, geometric machine learning and (higher) category theory.
