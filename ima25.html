Analysis of a Cuspidal 6R Robot

Here we provide supplementary Material to the paper "Analysis of a Cuspidal 6R Robot", 3rd IMA Conference on Mathematics of Robotics by Alexander Feeß and Martin Weiß.

Video 1 shows the nonsingular change of solutions described in Section 4.

Video 2 shows a motion between the same configurations as before, only that the fifth axis moves in the other direction. This avoids a self-intersection. The estimate does not hold anymore, as s5 changes sign, but computing the Jacobian Determinant reveals that the motion still is nonsingular.
